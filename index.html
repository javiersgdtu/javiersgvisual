BIO or How I reach the world of Data Viz.



My university background is in Telecommunication Engineering. During my Master I became more interested into Data Science, so I took the leap to the Data World. I have worked in a several IT companies where I have translated business needs into technical specifications and created solutions through analytics.
I discovered my interest in Data Visualization while working in my previous company (healthcare IT startup based in Denmark). Since then I have specialized into Javascript visualization. I have extensively used D3 & other Javascript libraries (echarts.js,...). Also I have worked with other Viz solutions such as Flourish. My data visualizations had been successfully used both for software integration, branding & business presentations.

I am passionate about technological innovation & communicating results to different audiences. I would love to keep applying my knowledge on topics I am passionate about: IT, social behavior, health, media, humanitarian causes & education.
